The Avantelle Story

Curators of Luxury

Founded in the heart of the United Kingdom, Avantelle was born from a passion for exceptional craftsmanship and sustainable luxury. We recognised that the pre-owned luxury market deserved better than typical consignment shops. Too many beautiful pieces were overlooked, their quality undervalued, their stories untold.

"We're curators who understand that every Hermès bag, every pair of Louboutin heels, carries its own unique history."

Our founder, a former luxury retail specialist, created Avantelle to bridge the gap between sustainable fashion and uncompromising luxury standards. Every piece in our collection reflects our commitment to authenticity, quality, and the timeless appeal of luxury craftsmanship.

We believe exceptional pieces deserve exceptional care, whether that's through expert authentication, careful restoration, or simply ensuring they find their perfect new home.
