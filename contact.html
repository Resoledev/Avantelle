Frequently Asked Questions

How do I purchase a piece?

Browse our collection and click on any piece for details. Use the inquiry form on the product page or contact us directly via email. We'll respond with availability and payment options.

Are all pieces authenticated?

Yes, every item is rigorously authenticated by our expert specialists before listing. Each purchase includes a certificate of authenticity documenting our verification process.

Do you offer authentication services?

We provide professional authentication services for Christian Louboutin, Chanel, Hermès, and other luxury brands. Contact us with photos and details for a consultation.

Can I sell my luxury pieces through Avantelle?

We offer consignment services for authenticated luxury pieces. Contact us with photos, condition details, and proof of purchase. Our team will assess suitability for our collection.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 14-day return policy for authenticity concerns. Items must remain in original condition. Contact us before initiating any return for authorization.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship worldwide with full insurance. UK delivery typically takes 2-3 business days, international 5-7 business days. Shipping costs calculated at inquiry.
